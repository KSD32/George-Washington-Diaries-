Washington Paper Visuals

Graphs

  • Yorktown Graphic
  • The Yorktown graphic is a representation of the references found within Washington's Diary Entries while in Yorktown. The markup done for this document has been divided into major categories: City, State, Place, People, Date, Britain, United States political units, Military Officers, politiccal units, military units and Native American units.
  • Indian Graphic
  • The Indian graphic is a representation of the references found within Washington's letter to Indian Chiefs.The markup done for this document has been divided into major categories: State, People, US military, Date, Cities, Britain, Places, Native Tribes, and US political units.
  • Timeline of Documents and Important Events
  • The timeline is a creation of the dates of the documents that were used in this project, as well as the historical events that ocurred in the time frame being looked at. The purpose is to give a visualization of the time frame in which Washington had written the documents. Red longer lines indicates the major historical events, where the smaller green lines are the documents that Washington wrote.
